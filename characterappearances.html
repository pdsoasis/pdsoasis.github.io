PDS Character Appearances

Coolia

In Panzer Dragoon Saga, Coolias are among the few domesticated creatures found in the Panzer Dragoon world. Hunters use them to pull caravan carts and Seekers use them as transportation across long distances, such as deserts. They can be stubborn at times and hard to handle. Their main source of food are Junio Seeds, not a good food for a Coolia.

Wise Old Coolia

This wise old Coolia will be your guide to exploring the many interesting and mysterious cultures of Panzer Dragoon Saga. He has traveled far and knows much about the world and its people. You should listen to what he has to say, maybe you'll learn something.

Young Coolia

Many young Coolias throughout the Panzer Dragoon games, have been viewed as taboo. The village code was to kill any young Coolia who was emitting a strange green light from their throat. One of these baby Coolias survived and mutated into the dragon, we see in all of the Panzer Dragoon games. They are funny little creatures, always stumbling over things and appearing to do little dances when they are happy.

Young Energetic Coolia

As the wise old Coolia takes you through the many different cultures of Panzer Dragoon Saga, a young energetic Coolia will tag along with him. This little Coolia acts as the comic relief, throughout the explanation of each culture. He can be obnoxious, clumsy, funny and down right crazy sometimes. Most of the time, he just gets on the wise old Coolia's nerves but nevertheless, he is curious to learn and hear what the wise old Coolia has to say.

An'jou

An'jou is the leader of the caravan. His is the caravans most skilled hunter. He protects his people from monster attacks. He also kills monsters and uses their internal organs for antidotes and medicines.

His appearance in the Culture of PDS

An'jou makes an appearance in the Hunter Technology section. He is minding his own business feeding Junio Seeds to a Coolia, when something unexpected happens.

Radgam

Radgam is an old retired hunter. He can be found in the Village of Zoah. His friends were killed in a hunting accident, so he mostly lives a secluded life.

His appearance in the Culture of PDS

Radgam makes an appearance in the Hunter Beliefs section. Lets just say, he's not in the best of shape.

Raul

In Panzer Dragoon Saga, Raul is a hunter for hire, that works at a traveling caravan. At night, he takes guard duty to defend the caravan against blood thirsty monsters.

His appearance in the Culture of PDS

Raul makes an appearance in the Seeker Technology section. He is observed from afar during a pretty awkward moment. I won't spoil it for you, you'll have to read on to find out what he is doing!

Gash

Gash is the leader of the Seekers. He is also called Skiad-Ops-Gash, but just Gash will do. He has great knowledge of the Ancient Age and all of its menacing creatures. There is much about him that is a mystery and he likes to keep it that way.

His appearance in the Culture of PDS

Gash makes an appearance in the Seeker Traditions section. He isn't very happy, because of the crazy antics of a certain energetic Coolia. If you want to find out what happens to Gash, you'll have to go see for yourself.
