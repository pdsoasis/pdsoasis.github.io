Disc: 3

Location: Village of Zoah (Holy District)

Characters: Edna and Quitor's Wife

Dialogue:

{Quitor’s wife:}

{Edna:}
What... where!?

come from the heavens.

{Edna:}
Is he challenging the High

{Quitor’s wife:}
Oh, really?

Are you worried about the
Imperial troops? Don't worry,
the Gods will protect us.

{Edna:}

seen in the Liberal District.

{Quitor’s wife:}
Yes... to sell blessings from
the forest to outsiders.

{Edna:}
Is that why the deliveries from
the forest are decreasing?
I'm sure the High Priest wouldn't
approve, if he ever found out.
