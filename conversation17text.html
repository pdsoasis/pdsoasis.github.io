Secret and Optional NPC Dialogue

 

Optional Conversation # 17

Disc: 3

Location: Village of Zoah (Liberal District) Paet's workstation

Characters: Paet and Bezer

Dialogue:

{Bezer:}
Why aren't they evacuating?

{Paet:}
They believe that the Guardian Fire
will protect them.
They've got so much faith.

{Bezer:}
What are you going to do, Paet,
if the Empire occupies this place?

{Paet:}
Then I'll work as an engineer
for the Empire.
If you've got your head on straight,
you don't need faith.

{Bezer:}
Are you really gonna give me this
airship?

{Paet:}
I'm studying the engine.
That's why I made it in
the first place.
It's just taking up space right now.

{Bezer:}
Why not leave this
town in that ship?
The Empire may be coming.

{Paet:}
Leave this town?
You got another place to go?

{Bezer:}
No, not really... But I'm sure
we'll be all right.
