Secret and Optional NPC Dialogue

 

Optional Conversation # 28

(Missable after going to the Forest of Zoah)

 

Disc: 4

Location: Seekers' Stronghold

Characters: Azel and Shella

Dialogue:

{Azel:}
So, you are Shella.

{Shella:}
My daddy gave me this name,
before he died.
Who gave you yours, Azel?

{Azel:}
The first person to call me
Azel was Craymen.
But he is with your father now.

{Shella:}
Then we are alike!

{Azel:}
Yes... We are

{Shella:}
Where were you born, Azel?

{Azel:}
I... don't remember.
But I was born long ago.

{Shella:}
How long?

{Azel:}
Long before your people existed...
In a time when there were no ruins,
and the Towers and monsters
were being created.

{Shella:}
Hmm...
