Secret and Optional NPC Dialogue

 

Optional Conversation # 2

Disc: 1

Location: Caravan

Characters: Kou and Fei

 

Dialogue:

{Kou:}
Silly girl...
Don't be frightened.

{Fei:}
But...

{Fei:}
Uncle An'jou is late...

{Kou:}
Feeling helpless without
the hunters around?
Don't worry. The scent rock
will last until nightfall.

{Kou:}
You'll never get used to traveling,
will you, Fei?

{Fei:}
But, mom...
aren't you afraid of monsters and
the Imperial troops?

{Kou:}
I'm more afraid of
running out of food and water.

{Fei:}
Mom...
What is it like to travel alone?

{Kou:}
What's the matter with you,
asking such a silly question?

{Fei:}
Well...
I felt like asking it...
