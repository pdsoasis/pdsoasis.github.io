Secret and Optional NPC Dialogue

 

Optional Conversation # 19

(Secret Dialogue)

 

Disc: 3

Location: Village of Zoah Viaman's House (Holy District)

Characters: Viaman and Quitor

Dialogue:


(Someone's talking behind this door.)

{Quitor:}
Vaiman...
Are you sure we can trust him?
Trust the outside, and...

{Vaiman:}
Yes, I know.
But, to rule, we need a legend.
And he gives me exactly what I need.
Besides, he is just a boy.
Lie to him, and he'll serve us well.

(Someone's talking behind this door.)

{Vaiman:}
It's about trade with outside...
I shall leave it up to you, Quitor.

{Quitor:}
Thank you very much.

{Vaiman:}
You need not thank me,
but I do expect a large donation.
