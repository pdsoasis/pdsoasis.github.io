Dragon Models

(Written word for word from Panzer Dragoon Saga)

by Jeremy Prusak

 

Defense Class
This form offers an improved armor class at the cost of speed. If all three gauges are full, it can cast a spell that enchances its armor.

 

Attack Class
This form has powerful lasers, but has reduced magic capabilities. If all of its gauges are full, it will automatically counterattack.

 

Agility Class
This type of dragon is exceptionally fast. In order to streamline itself, it must reduce its armor class. If all three gauges are full, it can cast a spell that increases its overall speed.

 

Spiritual Class
This type of dragon has increased magical capabilities, at the cost of its laser power. If all three gauges are full, it can cast a spell that replenishes a fraction of its Berserk Points.

 

Basic Wing

This type of dragon has no morphing

abilities and limited

spell casting abilities.

 

Valiant Wing
Acquired after defeating the Gigra. The Valiant Wing can change its shape as well as its abilities.

 

Stripe Wing
Characterized by a stripe pattern on its wings, this form of dragon was aquired after the battle in Georgius.

 

Panzer Wing
This form was aquired after defeating the Basilus. It is heavily armored.

 

Eye Wing
Aquired after defeating Atolm. Characterized by the Eye-like designs on its wings.

 

Arm Wing
Aquired after defeating the infested Grig Orig. The dragon's body has taken on a sligtly more human appearence.

 

Light Wing
After collecting all of the D-Units, and some literature, the dragon has taken on a new form.

 

Solo Wing
The dragon has taken the form of one of the ancient dragons that destroyed the Imperial Capital long ago.

 

Other

 

Floater
A hovercraft armed with maintenance lasers, its purpose was to travel through the enormous underground facilities in Uru.
