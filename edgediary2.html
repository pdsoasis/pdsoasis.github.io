Disc 2

Day 5:

The Caravan/

Eavesdropping on the Citizens of Zoah

I made my way back to the caravan after having returned from the Forbidden Zone. I went to Fei's tent to see how she was doing. At first, she was cold towards me, but as time went on and after talking to her many times, she bagan to warm up to me and started to talk. She told me she was no longer afraid of the outside world, because it was the same world I lived in. I told her I had to leave soon, she said she would miss me. I think she likes me. Night soon fell upon the caravan. I found Raul, in the back working the night shift. He asked me to do him a favor. He gave me an item called the Verato Pipe. He said if I found any survivors from the village Verato, to please give the pipe to them. I agreed. In the morning, I left for the Town of Zoah. There I was, at the great Town of Zoah. Many things to do, many people to see. The first thing I did was eavesdrop on the citizens conversations. If I saw two people together talking, I would stand far away and listen to what they were saying. It was a good way to see what was on the citizens minds.

Jared the Shopkeeper's Generosity

While in Zoah, in the Liberal District, I met the shopkeeper Jared. Upon examining his bookshelf, he generosly let me keep some of his old books that he didn't want any longer. The books he gave me consisted of: Dragon Book 1, Ship Report, Bible 2, and Zoah Bible.

Juba's Bar

I found a girl named Jeal, a waitress in the Liberal Districts bar. I gave her the Verato Pipe Raul had given me, and she happily warmed up to me. Soon after, I went upstairs to have a drink. The table to the left that I was sitting at, had "They killed Kenny," etched into its surface. I didn't understand its meaning.

Radgam's Quiz

I visited an old hunter called Radgam, in the Liberal District. I looked at his gun that he had sitting on a pedestal. It was in some ways similar to mine. He told me if I was to survive in the world, I should be knowledgable of the many creatures that exist outside of Zoah. He asked me to take his hunter's quiz, and I accepted.

He began by asking me, what was a safe area when fighting a Baldor? I confidently told him, it was the back.

Next, he asked me how would I go about killing a Lathum? I told him you just have to find its weak point.

Then, he asked how would I make a Nanyd Swarm scatter. I said, "just kill the Queen".

His next question was, "What is a characteristic of a Lazara?"I replied by saying, "They dodge lasers".

Then he asked, "Which do not live in the Valley?" I looked at him straight in the eyes and said, "Hoppers".

Next he asked, "What is summoned by a Pluder?" "Nanyds", was my answer.

"What is the Horned Stryders attack", he replied. I told him, "Paralyzing Shard of course".

His final question was, "which is stronger Magnata or Gelata?" My final answer was, "Magnata Urchins".

He told me I had done a great job and gave me a Tri-Burst.

Abners Request

During my Long day in the Liberal District, I met a man named Abner. While in his house he asked me if he could borrow 100 Dynes. I told him it was ok, as long as he would pay me back. He said, "no problem."

Bezer's Secret Hiding Spot

While walking around the Liberal District, I stumbled upon an unusual well. I decided to climb down it, and found a boy named Bezer. Bezer told me not to tell anyone of his secret hiding spot and he gave me an Amirkhan Ring.

 

Day 6: Fighting The Guardian Dragon

The Empire awakened a great evil called the Guardian Dragon, which Gash and I had to destroy. Gash told me that some creatures can't die until their job has been fulfilled. After a long and fierce battle, we finally put the ancient evil to rest.

 

Day 7: Back to the Caravan

I decided to see how everyone at the caravan was doing. It was late at night, but I went anyway. I found An'jou cutting a monster. He gave me a book called the Dragon Bible. Next, I went out back to go see Raul. I told him that I had found a survivor from Verato Village, and that I had given her the pipe. He said he was happy someone had survived, and told me a sad story about his destroyed village. He gave me 1,000 Dynes as thanks. I said good-bye to Fei and gave her the Amirkhan Ring, I received from Bezer. She happily gave me a Palmer Amulet in return. I could see she was starting to become attached to me.

 

Day 8: The Events after the Fight with Drenholm

After the tiring fight with Drenholm, we found two doors that had previously been locked, were now open. We flew through the hidden passages to find the hidden extra berserk class "Plasma Vortex".

 

Day 9: The Dragon Learns Astral Phantoms

After defeating Drenholm and acquiring Protect key # 3 at Uru Deep North, I decided to leave Uru and rest at camp. I gave my dragon a name and after petting him 6 times, I left camp. Upon returning the next day, we formed a special bond. To show his thanks, for taking such good care of him, the dragon learned Astral Phantoms, a technique that would surely help us out in battle.
