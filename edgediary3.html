Disc 3

Day 10: Return to the Town of Zoah

After my long and endless battles, I decided I needed to return to Zoah, to have a couple of Juba's illuminating drinks. Jeal told me as thanks for giving her the Verato Pipe, she had made it possible to have a discount on my drinks. I only had to pay 10 Dynes rather than 20.

Jephthah the Seeker

After my drink, I went upstairs and found the Seeker Jephthah. He tried to sell me a Tri-Burst. I asked him to bring the price down. 1,000 Dynes was a steep price for a Tri-Burst. His last offer was 800 Dynes and I gratiously excepted.

Quitor and his Daughter Rhoda

After my visit at the bar, I walked myself over to the Holy District. I met a man named Quitor, and his daughter Rhoda. Quitor sold me Zaal nuts for a low price, and I sold him a Macran Jewl . His daughter Rhoda gave me a mysterious music box called the Aronak Unit (Note, you must have a Panzer Dragoon 2 Zwei save file on your Saturn's internal memory, in order to recieve this item). It played familiar melodies from another time and another place.

Abner's Confession

After talking to Vaimen, I decided it was time to get my 100 Dynes back from Abner. I stumbled upon him in the Liberal District. He confessed to me that he wouldn't be able to pay me back. He told me to go to his house and take whatever I wanted from his junk pile. I agreed, and took Dragon book 2 from his junk stash.

Paet's Room

My next stop was to check how Paet was doing. After a long conversation about my travels, he told me I could come by his house at night. Night soon arrived. Anxiously wanting to see Paet's room, I ran all the way down a side alley, to the left of Vaimens house. Paet let down his ladder, and I found myself in the great inventors room. His room was full of ancient technological junk. He gave me an old book called Dragon Book 3.

 

Day 11: Saving Enkak's Life

I went back to the caravan once more, to see how Enkak and An'jou were doing, only to find Enkak had come down with Mechannia Fever and An'jou had left to find the cure. The people from the caravan explained to me that a Tobitama Rock was the only thing that would cure this deadly fever. After hearing this, I knew I had to help An'jou find the cure, as two people searching for the Tobitama Rock was better than just one. At Uru, after having returned the three baby Tobitamas to their mother, the group of them abandoned their nest. I found the infamous Tobitama Rock, within this nest. I gave this to Biacah and he made an antidote for Enkak. Enkak soon began to steadily recover, and everyting at the caravan gradualy began to return to normal.

 

Day 12: Radgam's Death

Paet had given me a very detailed description of Mel-Kava at our campsite. Before I departed to face Mel-Kava, I decided to pay my respects to my friends souls at the Excavaton Site. To my surprise, I found Radgam dead. He lived a hunters Life and died a hunters death. Upon examining his body, I found his unloaded gun. I took the gun back to Aldo's place, in the Liberal District. He couldn't believe that Radgam was truly dead.

 

Day 13: Selling Radgam's Gun to Jared the Liberal District's Shopkeeper

Since I had no use for Radgam's large gun, I took it upon myself to sell it to Jared, the Liberal Districts shopkeeper. He gave me a heavty sum of money, 1,500 Dynes to be exact.

 

Day 14: Return to Uru and the Underground Ruins

I made it a habit to return to places of beauty. I just had to return to Uru, as it was a place of unimaginable beauty. This soon got me into some trouble. The dragon and I had to face a formidable and quite rare creature, called the Kolba. After this battle, I returned to the underground labs of Uru one last time. It was here on B3F, that I faced another rare creature called the Absorborac. After the Absorborac battle, the dragon and I did a little more exploring in this mysterious place. We found a secret level called B?F, that we stumbled upon when falling down a hole near the Prototype dragons, in B2F East, to the far North. Throughout the winding corridors, we found an assortment of useful items.

 

Day 15: The Dragon Pup and the Destruction of Shellcoof

The dragon and I returned to Georgius to lay the ancient ship Shellcoof to rest. With a laser rank of #4, we activated all of the pyramid relics, one by one. This allowed us access to the inner corridors of Shellcoof. Once inside, a ghost of a dragon appeared, and lead us to a room with a dragon crest, similar to the one in the ruins where I first met the dragon. I examined the crest only to be blinded by a massive flash of light. A dragon pup had emerged from the crest. The dragon said to me, "This is my true spirit, the spirit of Lagi." The dragon pup followed us whereever we went from that point on. I took the crest with us for safe keeping.

The Destruction of Shellcoof

After finding what we needed inside Shellcoof, it was time to lay the ancient ship to rest. We began to destroy the outter engine rudders of the massive ship, and it soon began to deteriorate. With one final attempt to protect itself, Shellcoof began to grab us with a series of laser beams. The dragon easily destroyed the laser beams, and the ship fell to its watery grave.
