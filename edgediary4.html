Day 17: Returning to the Garil Desert and Excavation Site # 4

My Dragon had grown considerably strong. His laser rank was now at level 6, so we went back to the Garil Desert to try and destroy the ancient ship that was buried in the sand. The dragon succeeded, and we acquired an ancient technique called "Plasma Swarm." After that, we decided to return to Excavation site # 4, to have ourselves a little fun. To test out the dragon's new laser level, we destroyed all of the gigantic fans. It was a blast! Strangely we also received items from these fans.

 

Day 18: An'jous Thanks

I thought it would be a nice idea to check on Enkak's progress, so the dragon and I payed another visit to the caravan. Enkak was back to his old self, and was as lively as ever. An'jou had also returned from his fruitless journey, he had failed in his attempt to find an antidote for his son's illness. To his surprise, he returned to find his son had completely recovered, thanks to me. He gave me a Berserk Vampire to show his thanks, for saving his sons life.

 

Day 19: The Forest of Zoah

After the infested Grig Orig was defeated, we returned to the Forest of Zoah, and discovered a Villatuya plant. It looked similar to the Kuo plants, we had found in the Garil Desert. The plant was found to the far north. It was bright blue and gave off a strange yellow fruit. The dragon ate this fruit right away, and while performing a role, his speed increased to an even greater extent (Note:while flying, press the R button to do a roll, doing so will allow the dragon to reach an even greater level of speed).

Golia Hunter and Golia Tracker

After getting the Villatuya fruit, we were ambushed by creatures called Golia Hunters, and Golia Trackers. The Golia Tracker attacked us while flying near the 3 Naga Cocoons, found around the lower portion of the area. The Golia Hunter attacked us while flying past a little patch of vegetation located to the center of the 3 cocoon tunnels. These creatures nearly took our lives and I'm glad to be standing here today, to tell my story.

 

Day 20: The Dragon's Transformation into Solowing

Upon exploring the Red Ruins, found within the forest of Zoah, we activated four rotating artifacts surrounding a gigantic central shrine, that in turn, opened corresponding doors. We first entered the shrine's southern door, to find a white relic. We proceeded to destroy it. Next, we entered the shrine's eastern door, and destroyed another white relic. Destroying this relic caused a giant, central, white ruin, to rise upward. We passed through this ruin only to find it brought us to the Garil Desert, at night. The desert was silent and peaceful. There was nothing more to see, so we went back to the Red Ruins. This time, we explored the northwestern chamber. Found within this chamber, was an empty pedestal. I placed the Dragon Crest we had received from inside Shellcoof on this pedestal, and to my amazement, the baby dragon pup merged with the crest and my dragon! This turned my dragon into the legendary blue dragon, Solowing! My dragon's voice echoed, "Now my soul is complete, my name is Lagi."

 

Day 21: The Seekers' Stronghold and Jareds Gift


Once we were in the Seekers" Stronghold, we found Jered, the shopkeeper from Zoah. He had been deceiving us all that time, as he himself was also a Seeker. Since we sold him Radgam's unloaded gun, he gave us an Assassin gun part.
