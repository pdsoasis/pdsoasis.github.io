Juba's Corner: Information about the Empire
{Juba:}
The Empire is a group of power hungry mad men. They control a massive fleet of ships with the destructive power to level a continent. They are ruthless and only seek to acquire dominant leadership over all surrounding nations. No one can stand in their way. their ambition for ultimate power is unbelievable. If you get in their way, they will completely obliterate you, without remorse! They also have control over a special force called the, Black Fleet. Craymen, a military mastermind is their leader. I heard he went renegade and is now branded as a traitor.
