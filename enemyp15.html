Baby Sand Worm

Info

As we explored the desert area around the green oasis, we found a baby sand worm stuck in a quicksand pit. It was dangling around trying to hold on for dear life! I wasn't going to let the poor thing meet such a terrible fate, so I had the dragon blast it out of the pit. The baby sand worm was knocked to safety and I felt that I had done a good deed. Gash on the other hand, wondered why I saved such an insignificant creature and didn't just leave it alone. Well, I told him it didn't deserve to die like that!
