Rare Enemy: Sand Mite

Strategy

Upon returning to the Blue Ruins after having defeated the Gigra, we encountered rare creatures called Sand Mites. They could be found to the northwest, northeast, southwest and southeastern corners of the cliff walls surrounding the Blue Ruins. Their weak spots could be found directly in front of them. They weren't as weak as we had predicted. They were extremely difficult to kill! It took many continuous shots of the dragon's laser fire to finally destroy them! Since they were so strong, I thought it a good idea to stay and have the dragon fight them until he became stronger.
