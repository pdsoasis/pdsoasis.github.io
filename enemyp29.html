Rare Enemy: Dracolyth

Strategy

While flying beside these creatures, we were attacked by an onslaught of berserk blasts. Towards the back seemed to be a safe spot were they couldn't harm us. Moving directly in front of them, allowed us to spot their weak points. Before we could attack, the winged creatures shot multiple chaotic blasts, one after the other. The dragon took a considerable amount of damage. They were taken out with my dragon's Cleansing Wave berserk attack.
