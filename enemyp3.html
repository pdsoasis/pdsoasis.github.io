Pattergen

Strategy

When we first encountered these creatures, I tried using my gun. To my amazement it worked!! To bad it couldn't have worked back at the Ruins Bottom Floors, when my life was in danger. I actually destroyed one of the monsters with one shot! Is the dragon somehow supplying my gun with energy? When the dragon and I went in front of these creatures, they attacked us with green orbs. To defeat them, the dragon moved directly behind them and fired a series of laser fire. They were soon defeated.
