In Panzer Dragoon Orta there is a secret scene (or at least a homage to PDS) where you see the Heresy Program (A name given to the gold dragon by the Panzer Dragoon community. This is the giant golden being/dragon with four wings that you saw at the end of Panzer Dragoon Saga) talking to Lagi. The Heresy Program is not actually talking directly to you, but rather you are listening to a recording from the past. It tells Lagi (the dragon) if he chooses to go back to the human world without its power, he will indeed be mortal and would eventually die (They were originally one complete life-form). The dragon chooses to leave the Sestren Network and the Heresy Program tells the dragon to watch over our friends.

My theory and personal opinion is: This scene was meant to be an extended ending to Panzer Dragoon Saga and an explanation as to how the dragon was able to return, after the events of PDS. Another peculiar circumstance to take note of is: The Heresy Program turned black at the end of PDS and is now shinning gold in Orta. Could it be that it took over the duties of the Sestren Network?
