Controlling Edge the Main Character

In Panzer Dragoon Saga, you take the role of Edge, a young hunter whom is chosen by the dragon, to be its new rider. There are certain parts of the game where your not riding the dragon and have to explore on foot. Moving the directional pad or analog stick on the analog controller, will move Edge forward. Holding down the B button will allow him to run.

Moving the D-pad or analog stick up, down left or right will allow Edge to move in those directions. Holding down the B button will make Edge run.
