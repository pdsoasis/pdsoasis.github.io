Panzer Dragoon Saga Locations/Story Overview

Warning: Some of these Location descriptions

Give away spoilers to events that happen in these areas.

 

Ruins-Bottom Floors

This is where Edge's adventure first begins. It's also where he meets the dragon for the first time.

 

Above the Excavation Site

This is where the Empire mines valuable relics and ancient technology.

 

Excavation Site

This is where Edge's friends were killed by Craymen. It is also the site where Azel was found and awakened.

 

Valley/

Excavation Site #4/Canyon Deep Gultch

This is a place of beauty, with crystal clear flowing waterfalls and the site of Edges battle with the Arachnith. Edge also meets Gash here for the first time.

 

The Village of Cainus

Craymen led monsters into this village resulting in a feeding frenzy. The villagers were ripped to pieces. Now, it is a deserted village full of wandering souls and lost memories.

 

Garil Desert/Worm Lair/Green Oasis/Blue Ruins

A place full of Sand Worms and ancient ruins. It is at the Blue Ruins where Edge and Gash figh the Gigra, and the site where Edge's dragon transforms for the first time.

 

Forbidden Zone/Georgius

The place of the infamous Shellcoof's resurrection, a ship from an ancient battle of long ago. And that battle from long ago becomes a battle of the present, as the Empire awakens the Guardian Dragon. Edge must fight and finally put the horrible creature to rest, just like Lundi did, in the final battle of Panzer Dragoon 2 Zwei.

 

Inside Shellcoof

Once inside Shellcoof, a spirit form the past beckons Edge to follow it. It takes him to a large chamber. Edge discovers a dragon crest very similar to the crest in the Ruins-Bottom Floors. A little dragon pup soon emerges from the crest, thought to be the true spirit of Lagi. He follows Edge and the dragon whereever they go. After leaving the inside of Shellcoof, if your dragon is strong enough, you can actually completely destroy shellcoof, by locking-on to it and shooting the hell out of its engine rudders. Although, it wont be so easy, as Shellcoof will try to stop you!

 

Uru/Sunken Southern Ruins/Passage/Deep North/Underground Ruins of Uru

A place of sunken memories. Throughout the passage, you will find an assortment of unique and interesting sea life. The sunset here is truly beautiful. It is here that Edge activates the Black Ruins, leading down to the Underground Ruins of Uru. This underground lab facility is also where Azel and the the Light Wing Project was created.

 

Imperial Air Force Post

It is here that Edge must stop the Deathmaker from reaching Zoa or face the sight of its utter destruction.

 

Forest of Zoah/Inside the Forest of Zoah/Red Ruins

This is where the infested Grig Orig resides. Edge had to fight a series of invinsible Arangata to reach the Grig Orig, but the only way to take them out, was to destroy their roots found inside the Forest of Zoah. Also, inside the Red ruins, was another dragon crest. If the dragon pup was with you and you had all 12 D-Units, the little guy would merge with the crest creating Solowing, the reincarnation of Lagi. Two extremely rare enemies called, the Golia Tracker and Golia Hunter could be fought if you were patient and persistant. You may remeber this ememy from Panzer Dragoon 2 Zwei, it was a Boss in Episode 3 found at the end of the forest path.

 

Town of Zoah/Liberal District/Holy District

It was here in the Liberal District, that Edge first met the inventor Paet. Paet had vast knowledge of ancient technology and was very helpful throughout Edge's quest. It was in the Holy District that Edge met Paet's father, Vaimen. He was a Greedy man with great ambitions for power. He convinced Edge to become the protector of the town. Unfortunately, Edge couldn't protect the town too well as it was destroyed by the invinsible Grig Orig.

 

Mel-Kava/Inside Mel-Kava

A giant ship also called "Gods Carriage", creates fog and defends the path to the Tower. Edge had to destroy the engines within Mel-Kava to completely obliterate it.

 

Seekers' Stronghold

The base of the Seekers. The Seekers are a group of people who fooled the world into thinking they were robbers of ancient technology, when in fact they were actually a group of people set on taking back the world, and freeing themselves from the will of the Ancients. The leader of the Seekers is "Shadow Eye" or just Gash. Gashe's previous mentor was Lundi from Panzer Dragoon 2 Zwei.

 

The Tower and the Path to Sestren

Warning: Ending Spoiler

Sestren is the controller of all Towers throughout the world. If Sestren is destroyed the Towers will stop functioning, and man will be freed from the will of the Ancients. Edge goes off to complete his adventure, to defeat Sestren and fulfull his destiny. Azel opens the way to Sestren and Edge succesfully defeats the menacing computer network and saves the world. The Heresy Program leaves Lagi (the dragon), but Lagi chooses to go into the world in his weak state without the Heresy program, rather than be shut down and laid to rest. After his long journey, Edge is inevitably laid to rest as the Divine Visitor (the player) Which was keeping him alive since the begining of the game, leaves his body. Was Edge truly laid to rest? This is one question out of many that arise in the players mind, after beating the game. After these puzzling events, Azel endlessly searches for him. The outcome of her search is still a mystery, but we know one thing: Azel created Orta with either Edge's or Craymen's DNA. But that's another story and another game. Camp Locations

 

Camp 1

Throughout Panzer Dragoon Saga, you will be able to go to places called camps. At camp, you can pet your dragon, recover your dragon's health, and save. This is campsite 1. Campsite 1 is near the Valley. It is a very rocky mountainous area.

 

Camp 2

Campsite 2 is located in the Village of Cainus.

 

Camp 3

Campsite 3 is located near the Garil Desert.

 

Camp 4

Campsite 4 is located near the end of the Garil Desert. The terrain becomes less barren and is strung with vegetation.

 

Camp 5

Campsite 5 is located near the the Village of Zoah. The terrain becomes very rocky and barren once more.

 

Camp 6

Campsite 6 is located near the Forest of Zoah. It is surrounded by lush forest, as far as the eye can see.

 

Camp 7

Campsite 7 is located within the Seekers' Stronghold. It is a supply room that the Seekers use for storage.

 

Caravan Locations

 

Caravan Location 1

At a certain point in the game you will be introduced to the traveling caravan. It's a hunter colony of nomads, traveling from one place to the next. Large tents fill the area, with Coolias abound. Caravan location 1 can be found near the Garil Desert.

 

Caravan Location 2

Caravan location 2 can be found near the edge of the desert, revealing a more mountainous terrain full of vegetation.

 

Caravan Location 3

Caravan location 3 can be found near the Village of Zoah. The terrain is vast and barren.

 

Caravan Location 4

Caravan location 4 can be found at the start of the Forest of Zoah. The caravan has moved away from the mountainous region and is headed towards the forest.

 

Caravan Location 5

Caravan location 5 can be found within the Forest of Zoah. The caravan is surrounded by the green forest and its people have plenty of food.
