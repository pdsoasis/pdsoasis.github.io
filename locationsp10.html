Uru/Sunken Southern Ruins/Passage/Deep North/Underground Ruins of Uru

A place of sunken memories. Throughout the passage, you will find an assortment of unique and interesting sea life. The sunset here is truly beautiful. It is here that Edge activates the Black Ruins, leading down to the Underground Ruins of Uru. This underground lab facility is also where Azel and the the Light Wing Project was created.
