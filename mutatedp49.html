Tobitama

This strange creature can be found sitting in its nest in Uru. Many Tobitama children can also be seen flying around nearby. At a young age the young fly away. If all of the young return to the nest at the same time, they abandon it.The Tobitama's wings are connected to its head and it has a long protruding tail with a large ball at its tip. Its not very clear, but it seems that the Tobitama has one main eye in which it uses to see. It seems the Tobitama have returned to Uru because the water level rose.The eggs or droppings that they lay are called Tobitama Rocks. These Tobitama eggs or droppings, can cure most diseases.
