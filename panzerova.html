The Panzer Dragoon Anime OVA

 
After watching this Panzer Dragoon Anime OVA I thought to myself, "It's fairly decent." The greatest flaw I thought, was the fact that there just wasn't enough music in certain places. So, I took music from Panzer Dragoon Saga, and Panzer Dragoon Zwei and added it into the places I thought needed music the most. I did this with Pinnacle Studio 8 and edited the music in. Now I believe my edited version is much better than the original. So download it and find out for yourselves!
