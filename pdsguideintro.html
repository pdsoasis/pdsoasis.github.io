Introduction

Divine Visitor

Well, I've decided to make my very own unique, Panzer Dragoon Saga guide. I know it's kind of late, but I just feel It's my destiny to write one anyway.

You will be able to read Edge's diary entries one after the other, as he travels throughout the many places of the Panzer Dragoon Saga World.

Throughout the guide, you will be presented with numbered maps of every area in the game. Corresponding to the numbers on each map, will be the location of each and every item that can be found in that area. You can also view strategies for every enemy, as well as boss battle tactics.

At the bottom of each diary entry, will be an in depth, step by step, overview of what you had to do in each part of the game (screen shots included).

The overview is written by the Divine Visitor (the player, "me"!). So I hope you enjoy it, and get the most out of your play through Panzer Dragoon Saga, even if you've played the game a thousand times already.

For anyone playing this game for the first time, well, using this guide will provide you with everything you need to know about PDS. Have Fun, but be sure not to step in any Coolia's Dung!

Legaiaflame aka the Divine Visitor
