Zoah Technology

Zoah Piping System

Wise Old Coolia: The Town of Zoah has an intricate piping system. Pipes are strung about behind the walls of the town. Massive windmills are used to pump the water through the pipes to any destination needed.

Young Energetic Coolia: Hey, this windmill is cool! How does it pump the water?

Wise Old Coolia: WHAT ARE YOU DOING?!! GET DOWN FROM THERE, IT'S DANGEROUS!!

Young Energetic Coolia:HEEEELLLLP!! I'M STUCK ON THE WINDMILL!!

Wise Old Coolia: Oh brother!

These massive windmills pump water through giant pipes all over Zoah.

These water pipes must transport great quantities of water.

 

Ventilation Windmills

Young Energetic Coolia: The citizens of Zoah have massive amounts of food, preferably in the Holy District. Large windmills are needed for ventilation, so the food stays cool and fresh!

Wise Old Coolia: Hey what do you think you're doing! I'm the one who is supposed to be explaining everything.

A giant windmill keeps the food ventilated.

The Guardian Fire

Wise Old Coolia: The Guardian Fire, a relic of the Ancient Age, protects the town from monsters with its mysterious powers, and keeps the citizens safe. It may not be of their own creation, but it is the most advanced technology the Town of Zoah has.

The Guardian Fire protects the Town of Zoah during the night, from monster attacks with it's mysterious light.
